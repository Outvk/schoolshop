Our Story

Founded in 1995, Savory Bites began as a small family-owned bistro with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. What started as a humble kitchen with just ten tables has now grown into an award-winning establishment recognized for culinary excellence.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest seasonal ingredients, respect traditional techniques while embracing innovation, and above all, create dishes that delight the senses and nourish the soul.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries our commitment to quality, creativity, and the joy of sharing good food with good company.

- Chef Michael Richardson
